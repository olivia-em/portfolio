designing sound

This project was really interesting and helped me familiarize myself with Adobe Illustrator. The assignment was to record a sound and try to create an image which was representative of it. I work part-time at the campus rec center, so I recorded the sounds of the weight room. We weren't allowed to use color and each study was somewhat timed. I spent probably 30 minutes on each of the first studies, then developed them into the next three. We could either choose our favorite of those and do a third iteration, or we could find a way to combine all of them. I chose to pull ideas from all three because I wanted to emphasize how chaotic and layered the recording was. The last image is the final iteration.

Programs/Skills Used:

  • Adobe Illustrator
