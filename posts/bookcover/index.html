a redesigned book cover

In a design class, we were asked to choose one of our favorite books and remake the cover. As a classics major, I had taken a couple classes on the Iliad, one of which dealt with receptions. In the Philadelphia Museum of Art, there is an exhibition by Cy Twombly titled "Fifty Days at Iliam." We visited the exhibit during the course, and I found it really compelling. The Iliad is one of my favorite pieces of literature, especially because I have spent time translating it. In remaking its cover, I drew inspiration from a few of Twombly's pieces.

Programs/Skills Used:

  • Adobe Illustrator
